주요 참고 문헌

Finance / Econometrics

Variance Risk Premium 이론

  1. Carr, P., & Wu, L. (2009). Variance Risk Premia. Review of Financial Studies, 22(3), 1311-1341.
    • VRP의 표준 정의 제시
    • Risk-neutral vs physical measure 분석
  2. Bekaert, G., & Hoerova, M. (2014). The VIX, the Variance Premium and Stock Market Volatility. ECB Working Paper, No. 1675.
    • VIX² 분해: VIX² = E^P[RV] + VRP
    • VRP의 주가 수익률 예측력
  3. Hansen, P. R., Huang, Z., Tong, H., & Wang, S. (2021). Realized GARCH, CBOE VIX, and the Volatility Risk Premium. Journal of Financial Econometrics, 2024.
    • Realized GARCH 모형
    • 고빈도 데이터 활용한 VRP 측정

Cross-Asset VRP

  1. Heston, S. L., et al. (2023). Exploring the Variance Risk Premium Across Assets. AEA Conference 2024.
    • 20개 선물에 대한 VRP 분석
    • 자산별 VRP 이질성 발견
  2. Ornelas, J., et al. (2018). Volatility risk premia and future commodity returns. BIS Working Paper, No. 619.
    • 원자재 VRP의 수익률 예측력
    • Cross-market spillover 효과

Options & VIX

  1. Fleming, J., Ostdiek, B., & Whaley, R. E. (1995). Predicting stock market volatility: a new measure. Journal of Finance, 50(1), 151-178.

  2. Whaley, R. E. (2000). The investor fear gauge. Journal of Portfolio Management, 26(3), 12-17.

Computer Science / Machine Learning

ML-based Volatility Forecasting

  1. Carr, P., Wu, L., & Zhang, Z. (2019). Using Machine Learning to Predict Realized Variance. ArXiv preprint.
    • ML을 통한 RV 예측
    • VRP deviation 예측의 우수성
  2. Christensen, K., et al. (2022). Volatility forecasting with machine learning and intraday commonality. Journal of Financial Econometrics, 2024.
    • Intraday commonality 개념
    • Neural Network의 성능 우위
  3. Roszyk, K., & Slepaczuk, R. (2024). The Hybrid Forecast of S&P 500 Volatility ensembled from VIX, GARCH and LSTM. ArXiv preprint.
    • LSTM-GARCH 하이브리드 모델
    • VIX 입력의 효과

Validation & Best Practices

  1. Benhenda, M., et al. (2026). A Standardized Benchmark of Look-ahead Bias in Point-in-Time Financial ML. ArXiv preprint.
    • Look-ahead bias 벤치마킹
    • Time-series cross-validation 프레임워크
  2. Lopez de Prado, M. (2018). Advances in Financial Machine Learning. Wiley.
    • Purged rolling window validation
    • Financial ML best practices
